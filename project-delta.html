Project Delta

Sustainable packaging design for an artisanal coffee brand.

Material & Form

The core of Project Delta was sustainability. We sourced 100% recycled paper stock and used a single-color, soy-based ink printing process. The structural design minimizes material waste while creating a memorable unboxing experience for the customer.
